Welcome to Techlosopy!

The tech field is booming, literally!

According to research from Zippia, There are at least 3.97 million tech jobs available in the United States as of 2022. And it will not stop there. Second only to healthcare, the technology field is one of the most popular and high employment fields out there! When there are more people using technology in their everyday lives, there are sure to be a demand for people to help create those devices and softwares.

What are the hottest fields in Technology?

You might be wondering what is hot in the Technology field. Honestly, every path in the technology field is hot! However, here are the hottest fields right now!

Data Science/Analytics

Jobs in the Data Science and Analytics field are booming and plentiful. The average salary of a career in this field are high, with a Data Analyst making an average of $82,575 a year, and a Data Scientist making $103,892 a year. Data is valuable overall, and businesses/organizations use data to make better business decisions and increase their revenue and/or profits.

Cyber Security

Information is crucial for a business or even oneself, and an information breach may jepordize both, as the attacker can sell that sensitive information to anyone! Since information is sensitive and can determine one's life, cyber security is heavily needed to prevent any data breaches and/or viruses that may collect information without anyone knowing.

Cloud

Cloud has been arguably the hottest field in the technology market right now! With so many businesses and companies migrating their services to the cloud, they'll need specialists and architects to design, implement, update, and maintain those cloud services in order to keep the business functional!
